Welcome to Mars 

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System after Mercury.
In English, Mars carries a name of the Roman god of war, and is often referred to as the "Red Planet", 
because the iron oxide prevalent on its surface gives it a reddish appearance that is distinctive among 
the astronomical bodies visible to the naked eye.
